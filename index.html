Beagle Trivia

  1. The Beagle is a small to medium-sized breed of hound.
  2. They are known for their keen sense of smell and tracking instinct.
  3. Beagles were originally bred for hunting rabbits.
  4. They are friendly and curious dogs, making them great family pets.
